ABOUT US

Overqualified Housewives was started with one goal in mind - to promote financial independence and skill monetisation in Indian women.

Only 5.4% of urban Indian women make any kind of income. We, at Overqualified Housewives, intend to change it!

This platform will act as a catalyst in giving women their power back - the power called 'Financial Independence'
